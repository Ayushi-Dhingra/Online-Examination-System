ABOUT US

In this era,every single eprson and work depends on technology even the examination system. Online Examination System is technology-driven way to simplify examination activities like defining exam patterns with question bank, defining exam timer, objective/ subjective question sections, conducting exams using the computer or mobile devices in a paperless manner.

  • Online Examination System is a cost-effective , scalable way to convert traditional pen and paper-based exams to online and paperless mode.
  • Candidates can appear for the exam using any desktop, laptop or mobile device with a browser.
  • Exam results can be generated instantly for the objective type of questions.
  • It can simplify overall examination management and result in generation activity.
Platform is used to take online assessments , mock tests, and placement papers. It provides flexibility and provides certain features as negative marking, random questions and many more.
